Height map
----------

Put together some terrain generation techniques (interpolation between them)
to get different sort of mountains. For large terrain we could have high and
abrupt mountains and also a prairie / plane.

Constrain a depth to zero to form an island.
When it comes down to zero, it should not go up.

Ocean waves around island or lakes.
